Powerful Features

🚀

Multi-threaded

Uses up to 500 threads depending on your system to fetch assets blazing fast.

🎨

GUI & CLI Support

Prefer a visual interface? We have a Pygame window. Love the terminal? We got you covered.

🔗

CORS & Deep Linking

Configurable depths, same-origin deviation, and Cross-Origin Resource Sharing handling.
